About Me

NGUYEN Do Duc Anh is a PhD student at IMT Atlantique in France, working in the SOTERN team of IRISA. He received his Master's degree in Information Security from the University of Limoges, France.

His current research focuses on developing an automated attack response system, with a particular emphasis on using Intent-Based Networking and Microservices as key components of security configuration automation.
